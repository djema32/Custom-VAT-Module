Custom VAT Module

Display Product Price Including & Excluding VAT

Features

Automatic Tax Calculation: Automatically calculates VAT-inclusive and VAT-exclusive prices using the product form. Handles multiple tax configurations effortlessly.

Elegant Display on Product Catalogue: Displays VAT-exclusive prices directly beneath VAT-inclusive prices in the product catalog for a seamless and user-friendly design.

Elegant Display on Product Pages: Shows VAT-exclusive prices next to VAT-inclusive prices on individual product pages, improving transparency.

Automatic Activation: Once the module is installed, it is automatically applied to your webshop without additional configuration.

Benefits

  • Enhanced Transparency: Customers can see the tax breakdown, which builds trust and confidence.
  • Legal Compliance: Ideal for regions requiring both VAT-inclusive and VAT-exclusive price displays.
  • User-Friendly Design: Integrates seamlessly into your existing webshop design for a polished appearance.
  • Accurate Calculations: Ensures precision using product-level tax settings, even with complex configurations.

How to Install

  1. Purchase the module for the corresponding Odoo version.
  2. Add the module to your Odoo database.
  3. Refresh the Odoo app list to recognize the new module.
  4. Install the module from the app list.
  5. After installation, the feature is automatically applied to your webshop.
